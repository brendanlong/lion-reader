Here's what's new in Lion Reader since the last update:

- **Fixed unread counts staying in sync across tabs** — reading or marking articles in one tab now correctly updates the unread count everywhere else ([#797](https://github.com/brendanlong/lion-reader/pull/797))
- **Fixed article read state not updating in place** — marking an entry as read now immediately reflects in the article view, not just the list ([#793](https://github.com/brendanlong/lion-reader/pull/793))
- **New users are now automatically subscribed to the Lion Reader announcements feed** — so you'll get release notes and updates right in your reader ([#768](https://github.com/brendanlong/lion-reader/pull/768), [feed](https://announcements.lionreader.com/feed.xml))
- **Screen stays on during narration** — your device will no longer sleep while an article is being read aloud ([#762](https://github.com/brendanlong/lion-reader/pull/762))
