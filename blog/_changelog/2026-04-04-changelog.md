Here are the highlights from this release:

- **Fixed the Recently Read list** to only show articles you've actually read, and improved read-state tracking so articles are consistently marked as read when viewed
- **Fixed swipe gesture false positives** that could trigger unintended actions while scrolling
- **Kept the screen on during narration** so your device no longer dims or sleeps while an article is being read aloud
- **Fixed a date display bug** where articles 360–364 days old would incorrectly show "0 years ago"
- **Fixed sync reliability issues**, including missed updates during sync and saved articles not being included in sync events
- **Added an announcements blog and RSS feed** so you can follow Lion Reader news right from your favourite reader
